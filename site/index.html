Fundraise with shielded ZEC

Like JustGiving, but private-by-default. Set up a page, share your donation address, and show live gifts on your campaign or stream. We never hold your funds — donors pay you directly.

How it works

No custody

Donations land in your shielded wallet. We only hold a read-only view key to detect incoming notes.

Minimal data

No accounts or email. An encrypted UFVK, your public address, and optional page text — that's it.

You pay on-chain

Scanning costs ~$0.02/day, prepaid in ZEC. Grace credit gets you live immediately.

How it works

  1. Create a donation wallet in Winbit32 — we recommend a dedicated vault (2-of-2 with your phone) via the purse or vault wizard.
  2. Copy your UFVK + unified address from the Zcash receive wizard. Use a donation-only wallet: a view key reveals all incoming amounts and memos.
  3. Pick a page slug (e.g. alice-marathon), write your story, and fund a few days of scanning with a small ZEC payment.
  4. Share ziving.org/p/your-slug and add the OBS overlay for stream alerts. Manage later at /manage.html.

Privacy posture

  • UFVK encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM); decrypted only in the scanner process
  • No accounts, emails, or IP logging on campaign pages
  • Donation events (amount + memo) pruned after 30 days
  • Owner token shown once — store it yourself; we only keep a hash
  • Capability URLs: unguessable overlay id for stream feeds; public slug for pages

Why ziving?

🎁 Gifts go straight to you Donors pay your shielded address wallet-to-wallet. We never hold a penny — there's nothing to freeze, skim, or lose.
🕶️ Private for givers & getters Shielded ZEC keeps amounts and memos between you and your donors. No accounts, no emails, no tracking.
📺 Live everywhere Gifts pop up on your page and in a free OBS stream overlay the moment they confirm. Memos become messages of support.
🪙 Pennies, not percentages No cut of your donations. You prepay scanning at $0.10/day in ZEC — a $5 top-up runs your page for ~50 days.

Up and running in three steps

  1. Get a donation wallet The wizard can create one right in your browser (save the seed phrase!), or open a phrase, view key, or Winbit32 vault you already have.
  2. Tell your story Pick your page address, add a story and goal, prepay a little scanning credit in ZEC — your page is live immediately on grace credit.
  3. Share & celebrate Send ziving.org/p/your-slug anywhere. Gifts and memos appear live on the page and your stream — book a homepage spotlight when you want more eyes.

Privacy posture

  • UFVK encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM); decrypted only in the scanner
  • No accounts, emails, or IP logging on campaign pages
  • No KYC, reputation, or cause checks — pages are unverified by design
  • Donation events pruned after 30 days
  • Owner token shown once — we only keep a hash
  • AI agents can set pages up via MCP on mcp.winbit32.com
